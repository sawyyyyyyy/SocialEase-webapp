Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a practice session?

Navigate to Practice Modules from the sidebar, choose a scenario that matches your comfort level, and click to begin. Your progress will be saved automatically.

How is my anxiety assessment scored?

Each question is scored from 1-5 based on your response. Your total score helps us recommend the most suitable exercises and track your improvement over time.

Can I retake the assessment?

Yes! You can retake the assessment anytime from the Assessment page. This helps you track your progress and see how your anxiety levels change over time.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. Your data is encrypted and never shared with third parties. You can delete your account and all associated data anytime from Settings.
